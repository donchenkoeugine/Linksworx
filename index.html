About Us

Linksworx has been operating for over 7 years delivering automation advice, custom software development and engineering services to promote and facilitate adoption of various technology in container port.

We have team of experience software engineers, pre-built custom modules that allow quick deployment and integration services for yard map, vehicle dashboards, driver visibility and reporting.
We can handle any technology whether it is DGPS or GNSS, OCR, laser, sensors, RFID or any other acronym , we do it all.

"Above all, we like to keep things simple and transparent not only explaining technology (after all it is not that difficult), but also with value that will create for you."
